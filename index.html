Together, we serve with compassion

Empowering lives through care, education, and community support.

Founded in 2025, United Hearts of Bengal began as a small grassroots initiative to support undeserved communities. What started with weekend tutoring for rural children has grown into a mission-driven organization serving communities across Bengal.

Today, our work spans education, healthcare, women’s empowerment, and sustainable livelihoods. Guided by community participation and strengthened by dedicated volunteers and donors, we remain committed to creating meaningful and lasting impact.
