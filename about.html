Our Story

Founded in 2025, United Hearts of Bengal began as a small grassroots initiative led by individuals who witnessed the struggles of underprivileged communities firsthand. What started as weekend tutoring sessions soon grew into a movement for change.

As the needs of the communities became clearer, our mission expanded to include healthcare initiatives, women’s empowerment programs, vocational training, and sustainable livelihood projects—each shaped by local voices.

Compassion drives us. Community guides us. Impact defines us.
